Welcome to Paradise

In February 2017, I went to O'ahu, Hawaii to visit some friends and see the sights. My friends really spoiled me and they showed me all around the island, even though I was only there for 5 days. I definitely should have stayed longer. Fortunately I am going back early next year.

We went to Waikiki one afternoon. Of course the sunsets are beautiful, the malasadas are delicious and the karaoke scene is wild. But most of it is very crowded and touristy which is not my thing. It was worth visiting to hang out in the park and check out the city views.

Malasadas are basically donuts with filling, covered in sugar.

We then went to Kane'ohe Bay on the Northeast side of the island. We hung out on the boat and stood on the sandbar all day drinking beer. It was pretty sweet.

I think Switzerland is the most beautiful place I have ever been. On our Europe road trip, my friend and I only passed through the country for one day. But one day I will return!

We spent most of the day in Lugano, which is the more Italian part of Switzerland. There are many palm trees and the architecture is more Mediterranean style.

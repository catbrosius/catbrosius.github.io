Lincoln Center

I attended many concerts at Lincoln Center, where the NY Philharmonic plays. Tip: they give out free tickets online for select Fridays for people under 26 years old! I definitely took advantage of this.

Brooklyn

I wish I would have lived in Brooklyn. Tree-lined sidewalks, historic brownstones, the downtown Brooklyn Pier, Prospect Park...what's not to love? Fortunately I was able to cat and dog sit fairly often there for coworkers.
